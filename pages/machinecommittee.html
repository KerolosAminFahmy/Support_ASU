Machine Learning COMMITTEE The machine learning committee focuses on teaching various topics related to machine learning and deep learning. The topics covered range from the basics of data understanding and analysis to supervised and unsupervised learning, up to neural networks. The committee aims to provide a comprehensive teaching of the basic theories accompanied with practical tasks on the topics taught.
